Future Leader Summit 2022

Future Leader Summit want to prepare young leaders from all over Indonesia, we provide a leadership platform that will be filled with innovative and progressive ideas. We calls for great youths in reviewing various national issues that occur in Indonesia with the hope that they can be implemented sustainably in order to support the development of an efficient Indonesian nation.

Vision

The realization of the 2022 Future Leader Summit as a forum for inclusive innovation for youth revolutionary for the welfare of the development of the Indonesian nation.

Mission

Creating an active and appreciative work or internal environment, building internal and external relationships, implementing a progressive organizational culture, and optimizing inclusive innovation to develop the Future Leader Summit 2022.

Organizational Culture

Inventif

Demi terimplementasikannya inovasi baru yang diinisiasi oleh rangers maupun delegates, diharapkan Future Leader Summit 2022 berperan sebagai salah satu sistem wadah pembaharuan bagi Indonesia.

Apresiatif

Demi terciptanya lingkungan kerja atau internal yang harmonis, setiap rangers perlu memberikan apresiasi terhadap satu sama lain. Hal tersebut dapat membantu peningkatan kualitas kinerja pada rangers.

Kooperatif

Demi terwujudnya Future Leader Summit 2022, setiap rangers perlu bekerja sama secara profesional, berkomitmen penuh, serta berkontribusi secara maksimal dalam melaksanakan tugas-tugasnya.
